**Developer Critique**

1. **What did they do well?**  
    Their website is well-organized and the navigation is smooth. The implemented features are helpful and demonstrate that the group understands how to leverage APIs well. The overall experience of using their website is informative and intuitive.

2. **How effective was their RESTful API?**  
    Their RESTful API was very effective. The results returned from search queries, filtering, and sorting were delivered with very little latency and felt professional.

3. **How well did they implement your user stories?**  
    They implemented our user stories very well. They delivered what we asked for, and whenever there was a discrepancy in the requirements or a need to modify the story or its execution, they communicated effectively and in a timely manner.

4. **What did we learn from their website?**  
    We learned how to effectively use the Google Maps API and identified additional features that could be helpful for our users. The data sources they incorporated would also be useful if we chose to expand our own database.

5. **What can they do better?**  
    They could add more instances to cover a larger area. Currently, most instances seem to be from the Austin area, so it would be beneficial if they expanded to other metropolitan areas in Texas and eventually to more states.

6. **What puzzles us about their website?**  
    The total count displayed on the model pages is confusing. We initially thought the count referred to the total number of instances they had for the model, but it appears to show only the number of instances currently displayed on the page. It would be helpful if this were made clearer by labeling it as something like “Showing: 20 Results” or a similar phrase.
